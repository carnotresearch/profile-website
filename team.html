Our Team

Meet the people behind Carnot Research

We are a team of professors, scientists, and engineers from the top IITs in the country. We share a passion for conducting deployable research and innovating deep-tech products in the area of machine learning and deep learning, including NLP. Collectively, the team members have experience of over 120 years and hold multiple patents and papers in the top journals and conferences.

Founders

Prof. Brejesh Lall

Senior Professor, IIT Delhi

Prof. Brejesh Lall is a Senior Professor at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi and a prominent specialist in Signal Processing and Intelligent Systems. He received his PhD from IIT Delhi in the area of Multirate Signal Processing and has several decades of experience in academia, advanced communications, and the actual deployment of technology in real-world situations.

He has guided 13 PhD scholars and has been principal investigator in close to 47 sponsored research and consultancy projects with total funding more than ₹70 crores. He has been active in the areas of research such as object tracking and classification, depth mapping, multimodal signal processing and new communication paradigms.

He is the Former Head of the Bharti School of Telecommunication Technology and Management at IIT Delhi, has also been involved in the coordination of several important Industry-Institute Collaborative Research and Development (CRD) projects and set up an IoT laboratory at the institute.

At Carnot Research, Prof. Lall provides the deep-tech foundation behind our secure, on-premise, mission-critical AI systems, ensuring robust, field-ready, and sovereign AI solutions. He shapes the company's core research direction, translating advanced signal processing and AI methodologies into scalable, real-world deployments. His guidance ensures technical rigor, architectural excellence, and long-term innovation across all Carnot initiatives.

Col. (Dr.) Amit Oberoi

Strategic Leadership & Deployment

Col. (Dr.) Amit Oberoi has over 25 years of leadership experience in the Indian Army. He has held senior positions such as Commanding Officer, Senior Director, Head of IT and Cyber Security, Program Manager, and Instructor. His skills include computer networks, cybersecurity, data analytics, probability theory, and machine learning.

Currently, his work focuses on Natural Language Processing (NLP), knowledge representation, multimodal analytics, and AI-driven threat intelligence systems. He has contributed to international AI projects through the ITU Study Group 12 and has helped shape AI and machine learning challenges related to 5G and network systems.

At Carnot Research, Dr. Oberoi leads the strategic execution and deployment of secure, mission-critical AI systems. He ensures that our research and our AI solutions have a real-world impact in government, defense, and business settings.
