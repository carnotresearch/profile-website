Key Features

On-Premise Deployment

Data stays entirely within your organization's private network. Zero data leakage guarantee with complete data sovereignty and regulatory compliance.

AI Knowledge Assistant

Ask questions about internal documents in natural language. Provides cited, source-referenced answers with confidence scores — 94%+ confidence on responses.
