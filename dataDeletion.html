Data Deletion Instructions

At icarKnoTM, we respect your privacy and ensure that you have full control over your data.

How to Request Data Deletion:

  1. Log in to your account on our application using your registered credentials.
  2. Navigate to the Account Settings section.
  3. Select Privacy Settings or Data Management.
  4. Choose the option Delete My Data or Request Data Deletion.
  5. Confirm your request by following the on-screen instructions.

Alternate Method:

If you are unable to access your account or prefer another method, you can request data deletion by contacting our support team:

Email: contact@carnotresearch.com

Subject: "Data Deletion Request"

Include the following details in your email:

  • Your full name
  • Registered email address
  • Reason for data deletion (optional)

Our support team will verify your identity and process your request within 7-10 business days.

What Happens After Data Deletion:

Once your data deletion request is processed:

  • All your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers.
  • Any associated content or account history will also be deleted, unless legally required to retain specific information.

Please note: Data deletion is irreversible, and once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

If you have further questions about our data deletion process, feel free to contact us at contact@carnotresearch.com.
