Who We Are.

We are a team of 15 professors, scientists, and engineers from the top IITs in the country. We share a passion for conducting deployable research in the area of Artificial Intelligence encompassing machine learning and deep learning. Collectively, the team members have experience of over 120 years and hold multiple patents and papers in the top journal and conferences.

What we Do.

Carnot Research offers cutting edge technology research services in the area of computer vision and natural language processing. The key differentiator is our ability to provide managed research for serving your innovation needs. Our strength is the selected group of researcher, trained in the best institutes and with extensive industry experience, that steer research to realize/exceed your business goals.
